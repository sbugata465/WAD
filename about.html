GITAM is a deemed-to-be university with campuses in Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru, founded in 1980. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in disciplines like engineering, pharmacy, law, and medicine, with a focus on holistic development and industry-relevant education. The university emphasizes values inspired by Mahatma Gandhi, with a mission to provide a multidisciplinary education through global perspectives and a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship.

Visakhapatnam: The main and oldest campus, established in 1980, is situated near the Bay of Bengal and the Eastern Ghats.
Hyderabad: Established in 2009.
Bengaluru: Established in 2012.
GIMSR: The medical college is on the Visakhapatnam campus.
